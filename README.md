# Rice Purity Test – What It Is and How It Works

The **Rice Purity Test** is a popular questionnaire designed to reflect on different life experiences. It is widely shared among students and young adults as a fun way to see how their experiences compare with others.

Originally created by students at Rice University, the test includes a list of questions related to social life, relationships, and personal experiences. At the end, participants receive a **score between 0 and 100**, which represents their “purity level”.

This repository explains what the test is, how it works, and how you can try it yourself.

---

## What Is the Rice Purity Test?

The **Rice Purity Test** is essentially a self-assessment quiz.

It contains a list of questions about various life experiences. Participants go through the list and select the experiences they have had.

The final result is a **purity score**, which helps people reflect on how many experiences they’ve had compared to others.

You can try the test here:  
👉 https://rice-purity-test.online

---

## How the Rice Purity Score Works

The scoring system is simple and easy to understand.

| Score Range | Meaning |
|-------------|--------|
| **100** | Very few experiences selected |
| **70 – 90** | Common experiences typical for many students |
| **40 – 70** | A wide range of social and life experiences |
| **Below 40** | Someone who has experienced many situations listed in the test |

It’s important to remember that the test is meant to be **light-hearted and fun**, not something to judge people by.

## Why the Rice Purity Test Became Popular

Over the years, the Rice Purity Test has become widely shared on social media and online communities.

Some reasons people enjoy taking the test include:

- Comparing scores with friends
- Reflecting on personal experiences
- Starting fun conversations in groups
- Looking back on college or teenage memories

Because of its simple format and interesting questions, the test often leads to unexpected discussions and stories.
